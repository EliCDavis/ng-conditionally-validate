Filling out forms on some webpages is not always an 'all-or-none' proccess. Certain data fields may or may not need validation due to how previous entries have been filled out. This library aims to make conditional validation in Angular 4 an intuitive 'one-liner'.
validate(form, 'address') .using(Validators.required) .when('workingFromHome') .is(true);
